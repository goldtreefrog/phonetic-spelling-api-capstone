Help

Type in the big white box and then click any of the buttons to the right.

  • Read Aloud will speak your text back to you.
  • Check Spelling will show you the words that you misspelled and help you by showing you words you might have meant instead.
  • Delete will erase everything you typed in the big box.

Keyboard Shortcuts

For those who like keyboard shortcuts (also called "access keys" or "accesskeys"), below is a list of those for this program. If a particular access key does not work, there may be a conflict with a browser plugin or the browser's own keyboard shortcuts. Note that we created no special access keys for "Zoom In" and "Zoom Out" because it is already standard within browsers to use Ctrl++ and Ctrl+- for these.

How you use the access keys below depends upon your browser. In Chrome, hold down the Alt key while you press the access key (Alt+accesskey). In Firefox, hold down Alt+Shift+accesskey. In Internet Explorer, Alt+accesskey has the effect of clicking buttons but hyperlinks are only selected, not clicked. Press Enter to follow them. Wikipedia has a list of access keys for many browsers.)
