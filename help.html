Help

Type in the big white box and then click any of the buttons to the right.

  • Read Aloud will speak your text back to you.
  • Check Spelling will show you the words that you misspelled and help you by showing you words you might have meant instead.
  • Save Snippets will copy everything in the big box into a big blue button that will appear below. The button is called a snippet. When you click your snippet, the computer will read aloud what you copied in there.
  • Delete will erase everything you typed in the big box. If you do not want to lose it completely, click the Save snippets button above first.
